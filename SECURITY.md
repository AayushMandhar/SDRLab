## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of SDRLab seriously. If you find any vulnerabilities or security flaws in this codebase, please do not open a public issue. Instead, report them directly to the project maintainers.

### Reporting Process
1. Email your findings with a detailed description, including steps to reproduce the issue, potential impact, and suggested remediations.
2. The maintainers will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours and work with you to analyze and patch the vulnerability.
3. A security advisory will be published along with a patch release (e.g. `v1.0.1`) once the issue has been resolved.
